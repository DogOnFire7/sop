## 2.1 Area of responisbility detailed

| Callsign | Area Of Responsibility |
|:---:|---|
| VRMM_GND | Male ground is responsible for:<br>- Issuing IFR & VFR clearances (as detailed in section 3.)<br>- Controlling the apron area<br>- Issuing pushback clearance (as detailed in section 4.) |
| VRMM_TWR | Male tower is responsible for:<br>- The controlling of maneuvering areas<br>- Issuing taxi in / out clearances (as detailed in section 5.)<br>- Issuing takeoff & landing clearances (as detailed in section 6.)<br>- The controlling of all aircraft within the Male TMA |

!!! info "Information"
    In real life Male Ground only provides IFR / VFR clearances, and pushback clearances. However, if coordinated, it is not a problem for Ground to take over the responsibility of taxing aircraft from Male Tower.
