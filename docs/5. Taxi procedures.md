## 5.1 Standard departure points

| Runway | Medium (from North Apron) | Medium (turboprop) | Medium and heavy (jet) |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 36 | TWY D4 or D5 | TWY D5 | TWY D6 or D7 |
| 18 | TWY D4 or D3 or TWY C Holding Position if entering via TWY D1 or D2 | TWY D4 | TWY D3 and TWY C Holding Position if entering via TWY D1 or D2 |

## 5.2 Taxi instruction
Aircraft should be instructed to taxi to a holding point based on their weight category, as per section 5.1, unless otherwise requested by a pilot. Controllers may ask a pilot whether they have a preferred departure point, or whether they are able to accept an intersection departure. However, it is at the pilot's discretion to accept or decline such a request.

!!! headphone "Example taxi instruction"
    Sky Surfer 102 (DQA102) taxi to holdingpoint D5 via B4

    "Taxi to holdingpoint D5 via B4, Sky Surfer 102 (DQA102)"

## 5.3 Departure instruction
During taxi or takeoff clearance, the controller should provide pilots with their initial departure instruction. This should include their initial climb, and an instruction to fly via the SID.

!!! headphone "Example taxi instruction (with departure instruction)"
    Sky Surfer 102 (DQA102) departure instruction maintain 6000ft [on / via] [SID / ATISA1A departure], taxi to holdingpoint D5 via B4
    
    "Maintain 6000ft on SID, taxi to holdingpoint D5 via B4, Sky Surfer 102 (DQA102)"
